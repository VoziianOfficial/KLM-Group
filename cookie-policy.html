2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include localStorage, pixels, tags, scripts, or other browser-based storage and tracking tools.

Cookies can help websites function properly, remember user preferences, understand website performance, and support marketing-related functionality when those tools are used.

3. Types Of Cookies We May Use

KLM Group may use different categories of cookies or similar technologies depending on website functionality, analytics setup, and future integrations.

Cookie Type Purpose Examples
Necessary Cookies Support basic website functionality, security, policy preference storage, and user experience. Remembering whether you accepted or declined the policy banner.
Analytics Cookies Help understand website visits, page interactions, device type, browser type, and general performance trends. Aggregated website performance or traffic analytics tools, if connected.
Marketing Cookies May support advertising, remarketing, campaign measurement, or audience-related insights if marketing tools are added. Ad platform pixels, conversion tags, or campaign measurement scripts, if used.

4. Necessary Cookies And Local Storage

Necessary cookies or local browser storage may be used to make the website work correctly and remember your policy confirmation choice.

For example, when you click “Accept” or “Decline” on the policy confirmation banner, this website may store that choice in your browser using localStorage so the banner does not keep appearing on every page.

Policy banner storage key

This website may use the localStorage key klmGroupPolicyChoice to remember whether a visitor accepted or declined the website policy confirmation banner.

5. Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies or scripts may be used to understand how visitors interact with the website. This may include pages visited, general traffic patterns, browser type, device type, referral source, and approximate interaction behavior.

Analytics information is typically used to improve website content, design, performance, accessibility, and marketing strategy. If analytics tools are connected, they may be provided by third-party service providers.

6. Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies, pixels, or tags may be used if KLM Group connects advertising or campaign measurement tools in the future. These technologies can help understand campaign performance, measure conversions, or support marketing-related insights.

Marketing cookies should not be understood as a guarantee of marketing performance, leads, sales, traffic volume, rankings, or revenue outcomes.

7. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies or similar technologies may be placed by third-party tools used for hosting, analytics, security, communication, advertising, performance measurement, or embedded website functionality.

Third-party providers may process information according to their own privacy and cookie policies. KLM Group does not control every third-party cookie or external technology that may be used by those providers.

8. Cookie And Policy Confirmation Banner

This website includes a cookie and policy confirmation banner. The banner provides links to the Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, and Terms of Service.

The banner is designed to remain visible until you click either “Accept” or “Decline.” Your choice may then be stored in your browser so the banner does not show again on future visits from the same browser.

  • Clicking “Accept” stores your acceptance choice.
  • Clicking “Decline” stores your decline choice.
  • Clearing browser storage may reset the banner.
  • Using another browser or device may show the banner again.

9. How To Manage Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete stored cookies, clear localStorage, or receive alerts before cookies are stored.

If you disable cookies or localStorage, some website functionality may not work as expected. For example, the website may not remember your policy banner choice.

You may also clear your browser data to remove saved choices and stored website preferences.
